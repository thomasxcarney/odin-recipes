Vegan Mac n Cheese

Description

A vegan take on classic comfort food that doesn't rely on processed fake cheese for flavor

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Nondairy milk
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pasta
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Mustard
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Boil water for pasta in a separate pot and once ready cook pasta according to package
  2. Make a roux with the oil and flour in a pan over medium heat
  3. Add milk and mix together well while turning up heat
  4. Once roux and milk mixture is boiling, add in all the other seasonings, mix together well, and then lower heat to simmer
  5. Once pasta is cooked and drained, and the sauce mixture looks like the right thickness, mix it all together in the empty pasta pot and serve
