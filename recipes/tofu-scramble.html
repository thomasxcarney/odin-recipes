Tofu Scramble

Description

A classic vegan breakfast that's easy to make and flexible on ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Salt or soy sauce
  • Cooking oil
  • Turmeric
  • Black pepper
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Desired seasonings
  • Assorted vegetables to mix in

Steps

  1. Press the tofu to remove the excess moisture
  2. Hit up the the oil in a pan and then crumble the tofu in
  3. Add the salt or soy sauce, mix together, and then add the other seasonings
  4. Add in vegetables and continue to stir and cook until everything looks and smells adequately cooked
  5. Stir in nutritional yeast and then serve
